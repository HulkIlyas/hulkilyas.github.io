What the app does

Six screens, one vision

Overview

Everything at a glance

Total balance split across payment and savings accounts, with transactions and quick actions on the home screen.

Payments

Send money in seconds

Full breakdown per transfer: amount, recipient, date, purpose, and balance after booking.

Savings

Save with a goal

Create savings jars for Zakaat, Hajj, holidays or a car — each with a visual progress bar.

Card

Your Mastercard, always ready

Virtual debit card visible at any time. Add it to Google Wallet with one tap.

Donate

Give when you can

Donate directly to Islamic charities and foundations right from the app.

Invest

Halal-screened stocks

Every stock gets a Halal score so you only invest in what aligns with your values.

Overview

A clear picture of your finances

The home screen shows the total balance split between payment and savings accounts. Transactions are categorised, searchable, and exportable. Paying takes a single tap.

Savings

Goal-driven saving

Users create separate savings jars for concrete goals — including Islamic obligations like Zakaat and Hajj. Each jar shows exactly how much is still needed.

Investing

Invest with principles

The investment module scores every stock for halal compliance. Buy and sell directly in the app, knowing your portfolio stays aligned with Islamic financial values.
